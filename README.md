A professional PostgreSQL database project that models a real-world online retail platform.

- Customer Management
- Employee Management
- Product Catalog
- Categories & Suppliers
- Shopping Cart
- Wishlist
- Orders & Payments
- Inventory Tracking
- Reviews & Ratings
- Delivery Tracking
- Views & Indexes
- Stored Procedures
- Functions
- Triggers
- CTE Queries
- Window Functions
- Sales Analytics
